About Me

I lived most of my life 20 miles outside Chicago. As a teenager I found my love of computers and problem solving, teaching myself how to use a command terminal for no reason other than curiosity. After roughly 20 years living in the same area, I packed my bags and traveled around, living in Texas, Seattle, and even Africa before finding my new home in Indianapolis.

Recently, I have refound that joy I had as a teenager, by marrying together my love for creativity and problem solving by diving head first into the world of development. I am currently nearly half-way through the .NET development course at ElevenFifty Academy and can not wait to put my new skills to use.
